This is the home page.

5A Hostel is located in Banda. It is a student residency that is situated close to Kyambogo University making it easy and convinient for university students to reach the campus on time for lectures. 5A Hostel has well spaced and clean rooms that ensures that it residents are always comfortable. It also provides free internet access (WI-FI) that enables students access the internet for research and online learning. 5A Hostel provides the best security as it has CCTV camera systems installed on every floor to prevent theft and other malicious activities. We also have exceptinal staff that attend to all residents and visitors aliken i.e. the hostel custodian and the security personel.
